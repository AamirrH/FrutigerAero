🌈 Frutiger Aero | Windows 7 Nostalgia💻🪟


Welcome to a digital time capsule — a tribute to the golden days of the Windows Vista and Windows 7 era. This aesthetic, known as Frutiger Aero, was more than just a design — it was a feeling. A vibe. A mood.
✨ Back when:
  • 💧 Buttons were glossy like raindrops
  • 🫧 Windows were transparent, floating like glass
  • 🌥️ Skies were always bright blue with soft, dreamy clouds
  • 🌿 Desktops had rolling green hills that felt alive
  • 🖱️ And everything responded with a satisfying click

  • 🎵 The startup chime? Pure serotonin.
  • 📂 Opening folders? Felt like flipping through treasure chests.
  • 🖼️ Customizing your desktop? A personal ritual.
  • 💬 MSN pings, Winamp skins, Rainmeter widgets — this was the internet’s golden hour.


    • This project brings all that back.
